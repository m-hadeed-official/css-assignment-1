THE FLOWERS

Flowers are one of the most beautiful creations of nature. They add color and beauty to the world around us. Flowers grow in gardens, fields, forests, and even deserts. They come in many shapes, sizes, and colors. Some flowers are small and simple while others are large and attractive. Flowers play an important role in nature. They help plants reproduce through pollination. Bees, butterflies, and birds are attracted to flowers. These insects help transfer pollen from one flower to another. Without flowers, many plants would not survive. Flowers are a symbol of love and peace. People give flowers on happy occasions. Flowers are also used in sad moments to express sympathy. Different flowers have different meanings. Roses are a symbol of love and romance. Lotus represents purity and beauty.
